About the Competition

The Miathon is our flagship AI competition that brings together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems. This year's theme focuses on "AI for Social Good," challenging participants to develop solutions that can make a positive impact on society.

Competition Format

The competition will run for 48 hours, during which teams will work on their projects. You can participate as an individual or form a team of up to 5 members. All submissions will be evaluated by our panel of AI experts based on innovation, technical merit, and potential impact.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: $25,000 + Mentorship Opportunity
  • 2nd Place: $15,000
  • 3rd Place: $10,000
  • Special Category Awards: $5,000 each

Eligibility

Open to all AI enthusiasts, students, and professionals worldwide. Participants must be at least 18 years old or have parental consent if younger.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation (30%)
  • Technical Implementation (30%)
  • Potential Impact (20%)
  • Presentation (20%)
